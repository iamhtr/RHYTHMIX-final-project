"ÁI" - TLINH
A FRESH AND MATURE ALBUM

With her debut album titled "Love," Tlinh not only narrates the love story and personal growth but also showcases why she deserves to be considered as the most prominent female rapper of the second generation after Kimmese, following in her footsteps.

Once a name that stirred up surprises in Rap Vietnam Season 1, with a love story that made headlines everywhere, and lyrics that were once deemed meaningless... However, Tlinh didn't stop there but continued to release her debut album titled "Love." The Gen Z singer not only remains steadfast on her unique path but also matures further in conveying messages through the hurdles she has faced.
